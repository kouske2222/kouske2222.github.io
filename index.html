Research Interests

I'm studying about Microwave Rocket. It is a type of beamed energy propulsion (BEP) system and uses millimeter-wave energy to heat and accelerate thruster for space launch.
Microwave Rocket is a promising technology for reducing the cost and environmental impact of space access.

01

Electromagnetic Analysis

Electromagnetic analysis is used to investigate millimeter-wave transmission systems and plasma behavior inside Microwave Rocket.

  • Physical Optics Method
  • FDTD
  • Air-breakdown
02

CFD Analysis

Computational fluid dynamics is employed to examine the airflow inside the microwave rocket and evaluate the resulting thrust.

  • CFD
  • Thrust
  • Detonation
03

Gyrotron

Using the laboratory’s dedicated millimeter-wave oscillator, a gyrotron, we study the underlying physics of phenomena such as plasma propagation.

  • Millimeter-wave
  • Plasma
